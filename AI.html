What is artificial intelligence engineering?

Entry Profile

Students entering the National Polytechnic Institute, in any of its programs and levels, must have the knowledge and basic skills that guarantee an adequate performance in the level to which they apply for admission. Likewise, they must have the necessary attitudes and values to take responsibility for their formative process and assume an active position in the study and development of the projects and work required, coinciding with the ideology and principles of the IPN.

Graduation Profile

Graduates of Artificial Intelligence Engineering will work collaboratively in multidisciplinary teams in the analysis, design, implementation, validation, deployment, supervision and management of intelligent systems, applying algorithms in areas such as machine learning, automatic natural language processing, artificial vision and bio-inspired models; exercising their profession with leadership, ethics and social responsibility.
