What is bachelor's degree in data science?

Entry Profile

Students entering the National Polytechnic Institute, in any of its programs and levels, must have the knowledge and basic skills that guarantee an adequate performance in the level to which they apply for admission. Likewise, they must have the necessary attitudes and values to take responsibility for their formative process and assume an active position in the study and development of the projects and work required, coinciding with the ideology and principles of the IPN.

Graduation Profile

The Data Science graduate will be able to extract implicit and complex, potentially useful knowledge (discovery of patterns, deviations, anomalies, outliers, interesting situations, trends) from large data sets. Use the methods of artificial intelligence, machine learning, statistics and database systems for making senior management decisions, based on data and mathematical models on probable, desirable and undesirable behaviors, participating in collaborative and interdisciplinary work dynamics with ethical sense and social responsibility.
